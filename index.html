Favourite Technical Concept

I had the joy of joing the Armstrong Posse and learning all about Blockchain technology. After attending 3 of the spikes held on the topic I am still very unclear on the specifics of the technology. However I do have a rudimentary grasp of how they work and what they can achieve. I found the topic so interesting that I even invested in some Crypto-Currency that the posse leaders thought would be worth a lot of money some day. After investing $42 to buy a single "Ether" at the beginning of March, I have seen my investment rise in value a whopping $40 in only 2 months. That's nearly a growth rate of 100%.
